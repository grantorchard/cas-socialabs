#### Getting Started
1. Navigate to [VMware Hands-on Labs](https://labs.hol.vmware.com).
2. In the search bar type "1902-03" and press enter.
3. Click on the "enroll" button.

{% lightbox /assets/images/00-introduction/01.png --title="Navigate to Hands-on Labs" --alt="Using Cloud Assembly Blueprints" --data="getting-started" --img-style="max-width:80%" %}

4. Enter the email address and password associated with your myvmware account.
5. Click on "Login".

{% lightbox /assets/images/00-introduction/02.png --title="Login" --alt="Using Cloud Assembly Blueprints" --data="getting-started" --img-style="max-width:80%" %}

6. Click on "start this lab".

{% lightbox /assets/images/00-introduction/03.png --title="Start the lab" --alt="Using Cloud Assembly Blueprints" --data="getting-started" --img-style="max-width:80%" %}

7. Validate your browser by clicking on "run tests".
8. Assuming all is well, click on "start lab".

{% lightbox /assets/images/00-introduction/04.png --title="Validate your browser" --alt="Using Cloud Assembly Blueprints" --data="getting-started" --img-style="max-width:80%" %}
